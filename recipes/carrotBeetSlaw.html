Carrot and Beetroot Slaw

Description

A delicous bowl of coleslaw made of carrot and beetroot with a fenel seed dressing

Ingredients

  • 4 large carrot
  • 4 beetroot (300g each ) washed, trimmed, peeled
  • 1/2 cup rough cut pecans
  • 2 tbsp sultana
  • 2 cups fresh parsley leaves
    • ~Fennel Dressing~
  • 1 1/2 tsp fennel seeds
  • 2 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1 1/2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil

Method

  1. Whisk mustard, crushed fennel seeds, vinegar and oil in bowl add salt and pepper
  2. Grate carrot and beetroot place in seperate bowls
  3. Add pecans, sultanas and parsley to carrot, pour dressing and toss to combine. Add beetroot and toss gently
